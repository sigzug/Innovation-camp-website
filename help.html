About us

MyAttendance was a project created during

This project was developed to help Ethiopia with the problem of attendance in school. Its purpose is to collect data of when the students are attending school, to map and pinpoints areas where students are unable to meet up to their school. The teacher logs in with an pre-made account and selects which school and class they work in. The teacher then "checks-off" which of the students who have been in class. This is then stored in servers on national level and used to create detailed statistics on how and when the students skip school.

Help students is our primary goal!
